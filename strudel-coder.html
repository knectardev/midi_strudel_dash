Strudel MIDI Coder

Converts MIDI input to Strudel music notation

  • Play MIDI: Notes are captured automatically
  • Copy Musical: Get note names (c4, d4, etc.)
  • Copy Integer: Get MIDI numbers (60, 62, etc.)
  • Clear Notes: Reset the sequence
  • Spacebar: Clear notes
  • Ctrl+C: Copy musical notation
  • Ctrl+Space: Copy integer notation
